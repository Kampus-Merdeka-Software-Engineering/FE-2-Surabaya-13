Policies & House Rules

Sunset Oasis Hotel embraces guest-friendly policies that span various aspects. Regarding cancellations, guests can cancel free of charge up to 72 hours before check-in, but cancellations within 72 hours of arrival are non-refundable. Check-in begins at 3:00 PM, and check-out must be completed by 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out options are available at an additional cost, contingent upon availability. The policy concerning extra guests includes an extra charge of $20 per person per night for each guest exceeding two adults in one room. Pets are allowed with a non-refundable fee of $50, and each room can accommodate a maximum of two pets. Guests with pets are responsible for any potential damages caused by their furry companions.

The hotel strictly enforces a non-smoking policy in all guest rooms and public areas. Smoking is prohibited, and violations may result in a $200 cleaning fee. Additionally, guests are expected to pay a security deposit of $100 at check-in, refundable upon check-out provided there are no damages or additional charges. To maintain a tranquil atmosphere, the hotel observes quiet hours from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM. During this period, guests are expected to minimize noise. Hotel facilities, including the pool and fitness center, are accessible during specified hours, with specific rules to be adhered to. All these policies are designed to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay for all guests. Prior to making a reservation, it is advisable to read and understand the specific policies.
